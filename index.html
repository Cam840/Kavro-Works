About Kamran

Hands that know where the stud is.

I started Kavro Works because too many people told me the same story — a TV crooked on the wall, a shelf that pulled from drywall, a mirror never quite straight.

Every job gets careful measuring, the right anchors for your wall type, cables tucked clean, and a tidy workspace when I leave. No shortcuts. If it's not level, I'm not done.

Based in Atlanta, serving homes and apartments across the metro. I show up on time, treat your home like my own, and stand behind the work.
