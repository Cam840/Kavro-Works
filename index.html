About Kamran

Hands that know where the stud is.

I started Kavro Works because too many people told me the same story — a TV crooked on the wall, a shelf that pulled out of drywall, furniture built wrong the first time.

Every job gets careful measuring, the right hardware for your specific wall, and a clean workspace when I leave. No shortcuts. If it's not level, I'm not done.
